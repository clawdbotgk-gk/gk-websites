What I Actually Do

No 47-page proposals. No buzzwords. Here's what you get when you work with me.

Most Popular

Custom Web Design

Your website designed from scratch — not a template with your logo slapped on. I'll work with you to understand your business, your customers, and what you actually need, then design something that looks great and does a job. Every site is mobile-first, fast-loading, and built so you can be proud to share the link.

  • Custom design mockups you approve first
  • Mobile-responsive — works on every screen
  • Clear calls-to-action
  • Design that reflects your brand

WordPress Development

Most of the sites I build run on WordPress — and for good reason. It powers over 40% of the web, it's flexible, and most importantly, you can update it yourself without ringing me every time you need to change a phone number. I build with Elementor so editing your own pages feels as easy as using a Word document.

  • WordPress + Elementor
  • Training so you can update yourself
  • Speed-optimised
  • Security hardened

E-Commerce Stores

Whether you're selling 10 products or 10,000, I'll set up a store that handles the lot — products, payments, shipping, the works. I build on both Shopify and WooCommerce depending on what suits your business best.

  • Full store setup with products
  • Payment processing (Stripe, PayPal)
  • Shipping & tax configuration
  • Inventory management

Maintenance & Support

Websites aren't "set and forget." WordPress needs updates, plugins need patching, and things occasionally break. I offer straightforward monthly plans so you don't have to worry about any of it.

About

Building Websites That Actually Work

I'm Gervis — a web developer based in Belfast, Northern Ireland. I've been building websites for over 15 years.

I started GK Websites because I got tired of agencies that overcomplicate things. You know the type — endless jargon, confusing processes, and websites that are harder to update than they should be.

My approach is simple: build websites that are fast, easy to manage, and actually help your business grow. No fluff, no tech speak, just solid web development.

I work with small and medium businesses across Northern Ireland and the UK, focusing on websites and web apps that deliver real value.
